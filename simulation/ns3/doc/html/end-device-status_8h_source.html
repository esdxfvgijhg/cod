53 /*
54  * Diagram of the end-device-status data structure. One instance of this class
55  * for each ED, that will be identified by its address.
56  *
57  * Public Access:
58  *
59  * (ED address) --- Current device parameters:
60  * - First Receive Window SF and DRRe
61  * - First Receive Window frequency
62  * - Second Window SF and DR
63  * - Second Receive Window frequency
64  * --- Reply
65  * - Need for reply (true/false)
66  * - Updated reply
67  * --- Received Packets
68  * - Received packets list (see below).
69  *
70  *
71  * Private Access:
72  *
73  * (Received packets list) - List of gateways that received the packet (see below)
74  * - SF of the received packet
75  * - Frequency of the received packet
76  * - Bandwidth of the received packet
77  *
78  * (Gateway list) - Time at which the packet was received
79  * - Reception power
