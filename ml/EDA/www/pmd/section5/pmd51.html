Detailed Information on Prediction This section details some of the different types of predictions that can be made using the various process models whose development is discussed in Section 4.1 through Section 4.4. Computational formulas or algorithms are given for each different type of estimation or prediction, along with simulation examples showing its probabilisitic interpretation. An introduction to the different types of estimation and prediction can be found in Section 4.1.3.1. A brief description of estimation and prediction versus the other uses of process models is given in Section 4.1.3.
