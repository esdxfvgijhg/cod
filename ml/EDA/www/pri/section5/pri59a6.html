How to Interpret: Optimal Curve
Corresponds to ideal optimum value The optimal curve is the curve on the contour plot that corresponds to the ideal optimum value.
Defective springs example For the defective springs data, we search for the Y = 100 contour curve. As determined in the steepest ascent/descent section, the Y = 90 curve is immediately outside the (+, +) point. The next curve to the right is the Y = 95 curve, and the next curve beyond that is the Y = 100 curve. This is the optimal response curve.
